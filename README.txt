Connect directly to your GivEnergy inverter over your local network for real-time monitoring and control. Track solar generation, battery state, and grid import/export in Homey's Energy tab. Adjust inverter modes, charge and discharge schedules, and rates using Homey Flows.

This is an unofficial community app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or related to GivEnergy Ltd. Only GivEnergy solar inverters and batteries are supported — EV chargers are not supported.
